Tracks are never finished

I recently lost a hard drive with around 300 tracks I made since 2010. 98% of those were unreleased, or never heard by anyone except me and collaborators/friends. I never played them in my DJ sets.
To avoid the feeling of losing unfinished work, I decided to be more free with how I dealt with making music and not get attached to the tracks. Now I just want to bounce them, put them up and move forward.
